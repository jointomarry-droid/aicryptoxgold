Frequently Asked Questions

What drives the price of gold today? expand_more

Gold prices are currently influenced by US inflation data, Federal Reserve interest rate policies, and geopolitical tensions. As a safe-haven asset, uncertainty in traditional equities often drives institutional capital into gold.

What is the difference between spot price and futures? expand_more

The spot price (XAU/USD) is the current price for immediate physical delivery. Futures contracts represent an agreement to buy or sell gold at a specific price on a future date, reflecting market expectations.
